About


I'm a recent Stanford graduate with an M.S. in Environmental Engineering and Science. I founded a startup HighTide Intelligence with two of my former students in the Future Bay Initiative. We’re creating actionable sea level rise analyses. I also currently support Professor Gabrielle Hecht in the Stanford History Department with her research on global mining waste.

Additionally, I'm an audiovisual artist. Under the name Slow Proteo I've played shows across the U.S. and Mexico. I've performed with Stanford Laptop Orchestra at Stanford's Bing Concert Hall and exhibited visual art with Stanford ArtX. I also run sound and visuals for the Fern Gully Productions in Santa Cruz, California — an outdoor concert series. Previously, I worked on event production at the SUNY Buffalo Center for the Arts.
